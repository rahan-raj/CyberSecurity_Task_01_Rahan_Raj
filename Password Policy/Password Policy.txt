Password Policy (5 Best Practices)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1) Use at least 12 characters — longer passwords are exponentially harder to brute-force.
2) Use a mix of character types — combine uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters (e.g., My$ecure#Pass2024).
3) Never reuse passwords — every account must have a unique password to limit breach exposure.
4) Avoid personal information — do not use your name, date of birth, phone number, or any guessable detail.
5) Enable MFA on every account that supports it — even a weak password is harder to exploit with MFA active.
